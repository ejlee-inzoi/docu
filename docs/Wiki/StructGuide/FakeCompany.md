# 📘 FakeCompany Data Guide

## 🧩 Struct: FB1FakeCompanyTableRow

Defines a set of fake company associations for characters or scripted behaviors.

### Properties

- **Id** (`FName`): Unique identifier for this fake company set.  
- **FakeCompanies** (`TArray<FB1FakeCompanyInfo>`): List of associated fake companies and sites.
