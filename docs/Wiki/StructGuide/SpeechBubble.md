## 🧩 Struct: FB1SpeechBubbleTableRow

Defines speech bubble data sets used for emotion or interaction expression in-game.

### Properties

- **Id** (`FName`): Unique identifier for this speech bubble group.  
- **SpeechBubbleData** (`TArray<FB1SpeechBubbleData>`): List of speech bubbles with text or icons.
