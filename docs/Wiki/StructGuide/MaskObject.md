# 📘 MaskObject Data Guide

## 🧩 Struct: FB1MaskObjectTableRow

Defines a texture mask asset used for applying material effects or visual customization to objects.

### Properties

- **Id** (`FName`): Unique identifier for the mask object.  
- **MaskTexture** (`TSoftObjectPtr<UTexture>`): Texture mask used for material masking or visual blending.
