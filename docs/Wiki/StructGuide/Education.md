# 📘 Education Data Guide

## 🧩 Struct: FB1EducationTableRow

Defines education data for various growth stages and their job associations.

### Properties

- **Id** (`FName`): Unique identifier for this education configuration.  
- **EducationDataList** (`TArray<FB1EducationTableData>`): List of education entries with type, growth step, and resulting jobs.
