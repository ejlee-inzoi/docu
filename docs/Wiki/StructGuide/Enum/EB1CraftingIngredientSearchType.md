## 🧾 Enum: EB1CraftingIngredientSearchType

Defines how ingredients are searched or selected during a crafting process.

- **None**: No search method is specified.  
- **Inventory**: Ingredients are searched from the character's inventory.  
- **TargetFilter**: Searches based on predefined target filter criteria.  
- **SkillCheck**: Filters ingredients based on skill-related checks.
