## 🧾 Enum: EB1EmploymentForm

Defines the form or type of employment associated with a job.

- **Invalid**: Represents an undefined or invalid employment form.  
- **None**: No employment form is set.  
- **FullTime**: Full-time employment.  
- **PartTime**: Part-time employment.  
- **Freelancer**: Independent contractor or freelance work.
