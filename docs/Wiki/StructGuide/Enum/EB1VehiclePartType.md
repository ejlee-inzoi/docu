## 🎛️ Enum: EB1VehiclePartType

Enumerates various vehicle parts that can be animated or interacted with.

- **Invalid**: Indicates an invalid or uninitialized value.  
- **DriverWindow**: The driver-side window.  
- **Passenger0Window**: The front passenger-side window.  
- **Passenger1Window**: The first rear passenger window.  
- **Passenger2Window**: The second rear passenger window.  
- **Trunk**: The vehicle's trunk compartment.  
- **ConvertibleRoof**: The retractable roof of a convertible car.  
- **Wiper**: The windshield wiper.  
- **HazzardLights**: The hazard (emergency) lights.  
- **LeftTurnSignalLight**: The left turn signal.  
- **RightTurnSignalLight**: The right turn signal.  
- **HeadLights**: The front-facing headlights.  
- **RunningLights**: The general running lights of the vehicle.
