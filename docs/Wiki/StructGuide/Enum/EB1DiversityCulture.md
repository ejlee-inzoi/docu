## 🌍 Enum: EB1DiversityCulture

Represents cultural backgrounds used for localization, naming, and visual diversity.

- **Invalid**: Unspecified or undefined culture.  
- **NorthAmerica**: Represents North American cultural background.  
- **Korea**: Represents Korean cultural background.  
- **Japan**: Represents Japanese cultural background.  
- **China**: Represents Chinese cultural background.
