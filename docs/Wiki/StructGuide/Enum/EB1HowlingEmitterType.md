## 🎛️ Enum: EB1HowlingEmitterType

Represents the source of a howling event, often used in AI or social propagation.

- **Base**: Default emitter type.  
- **VehicleAccident**: Emitter for a vehicle collision howling.  
- **Trend**: Emitter for trend-related propagation.  
- **Invalid**: Represents an invalid emitter.
