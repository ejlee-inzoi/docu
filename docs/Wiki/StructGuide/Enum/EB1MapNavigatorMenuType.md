## 🎛️ Enum: EB1MapNavigatorMenuType

Defines available menus in the map navigator interface.

- **Invalid**: No menu selected.  
- **Family**: Family management.  
- **CityEnvironment**: City environment control.  
- **Timeline**: Schedule or time management.  
- **CityTheme**: City theme settings.  
- **Weather**: Weather controls.  
- **SiteEdit**: Site editing tools.  
- **CityBalance**: City balance and stability.  
- **PhotoStudio**: Photo studio.  
- **CutsceneStudio**: Cutscene studio.  
- **DanceStudio**: Dance studio.  
- **KarmaReport**: Karma report overview.  
- **Sign**: Signboard or billboard menu.  
- **Tree**: Tree placement/editing.  
- **Grass**: Grass placement/editing.  
- **Decoration**: Decoration tools.  
- **Animal**: Animal-related options.  
- **SpecialEffect**: Special visual effects menu.
