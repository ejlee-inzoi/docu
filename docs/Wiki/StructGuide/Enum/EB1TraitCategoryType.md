## 🎛️ Enum: EB1TraitCategoryType

Classifies traits into broad categories for behavior and role assignment.

- **None**: Indicates no category.  
- **Nature**: Represents natural-born traits.  
- **Perspective**: Relates to viewpoint or value system.  
- **Ambition**: Reflects a character's ambition type.  
- **HopeLife**: Tied to long-term life goals or hope system.
