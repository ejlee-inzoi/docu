## 🏅 Enum: EB1Quality

Represents the quality level of an item, task, or result in the game.  
Used to rate outcomes or characteristics from poor to excellent.

- **None** (`0`): No quality assigned.
- **Bad**: Very poor quality.
- **Fair**: Acceptable, but below average quality.
- **Good**: Solid, above-average quality.
- **High**: Excellent quality, near the top tier.
- **Superb**: Top-tier quality, the best achievable.
