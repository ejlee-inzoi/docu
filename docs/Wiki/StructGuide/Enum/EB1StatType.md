## 🎛️ Enum: EB1StatType

Classifies the type of stat tracked for gameplay systems.

- **Invalid**: Indicates an invalid or uninitialized stat type.  
- **Motive**: Basic needs (e.g., hunger, hygiene, sleep, etc.).  
- **Hobby**: Pertains to character hobbies and leisure.(Currently unused)  
- **Work**: Associated with work or job-related performance.(Currently unused)
- **Visitor**: Tracks behavior or state of visitors.(Currently unused)
- **Weathering**: Represents wear, age, or environmental damage.  
- **Object**: Applies to objects or structures.(Currently unused)
