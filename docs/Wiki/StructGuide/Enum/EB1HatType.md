## 🎛️ Enum: EB1HatType

Defines various types of hats available in customization.

- **Default**: No hat or default headwear.  
- **BallCap**: Baseball cap.  
- **Fedora**: Fedora-style hat.  
- **Beanie**: Knit cap or beanie.  
- **Helmet**: Protective helmet.  
- **Durag**: Close-fitting headwrap.  
- **MAX**: Internal limit marker.
