## 😊 Enum: EB1EmotionType

Defines detailed emotional states used for characters in simulations.

- **None**: No active emotion.  
- **Normal**: Default or neutral mood.  
- **Sleep**: Feeling sleepy or dozing.  
- **Preoccupied**: Deeply focused or immersed.  
- **Curious**: Interested or inquisitive.  
- **Convinced**: Confident or assertive.  
- **Excited**: Energetic and thrilled.  
- **Enchanting**: Feeling charming or seductive.  
- **Funny**: Amused or entertained.  
- **Calm**: Peaceful and relaxed.  
- **Touched**: Emotionally moved.  
- **Surprised**: Caught off guard or amazed.  
- **Bored**: Feeling uninterested or dull.  
- **Tired**: Lacking energy or motivation.  
- **Sad**: Feeling down or sorrowful.  
- **Uncomfortable**: Physically or emotionally uneasy.  
- **Uneasy**: Anxious or tense.  
- **Cringing**: Embarrassed or awkward.  
- **Irritated**: Angry or annoyed.  
- **Worried**: Afraid or concerned.
