## 🍱 Enum: EB1CalorieType

Represents the calorie level of food, used in dietary or metabolism-related systems.

- **None**: No calorie information is specified.  
- **Zero**: Contains zero calories.  
- **Low**: Low-calorie item, contributes minimal energy.  
- **Middle**: Medium-calorie item, moderate energy supply.  
- **High**: High-calorie item, contributes significant energy.
