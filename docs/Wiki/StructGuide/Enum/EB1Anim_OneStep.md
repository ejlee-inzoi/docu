## 🎛️ Enum: EB1Anim_OneStep

Represents discrete single-step animation directions for character movement.

- **None**: No specific one-step animation.
- **Forward**: Step forward.
- **Back**: Step backward.
- **L_90**: Step to the left by 90 degrees.
- **R_90**: Step to the right by 90 degrees.
- **MAX**: Sentinel value marking the upper bound (not used directly).
