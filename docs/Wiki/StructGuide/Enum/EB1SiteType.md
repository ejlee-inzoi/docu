## 🎛️ Enum: EB1SiteType

Represents the category or functional role of a site within the game world.

- **Invalid**: Indicates an invalid or uninitialized value.  
- **Residence**: A residential site where characters live.  
- **Public**: A public location, such as a park or library.  
- **Business**: A commercial or business site.  
- **Subway**: A subway station or terminal.  
- **OutsideResidence**: An exterior space around a residence.  
- **Override**: A site that overrides standard definitions.  
- **Street**: A street or road segment.  
- **Bus**: A public bus vehicle treated as a site.  
- **MyVehicle**: A personal vehicle treated as a movable site.
