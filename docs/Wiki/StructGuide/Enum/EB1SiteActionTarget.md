## 🎛️ Enum: EB1SiteActionTarget

Represents categorized options used throughout simulation and logic systems.

- **Invalid**: Indicates an invalid or uninitialized value.  
- **Objects**: Refers to objects within the site.  
- **Inventory**: Refers to inventory or stored contents.  
- **Visitors**: Refers to visiting characters or NPCs.  
- **ResidanceFamilyMembers**: Refers to family members residing at the site.
