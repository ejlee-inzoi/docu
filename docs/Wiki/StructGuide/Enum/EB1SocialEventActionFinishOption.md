## ✅ Enum: EB1SocialEventActionFinishOption

Determines the condition under which a social event action is considered finished.

- **None** (`0`): No completion condition specified.
- **OneFinish**: The event finishes when one participant completes their action.
- **HalfFinish**: The event finishes when half of the participants have completed their actions.
- **AllFinish**: The event finishes when all participants have completed their actions.
