## 🎛️ Enum: EB1RelationshipChemistry

Represents the overall chemistry or compatibility between two characters.

- **Invalid**: Chemistry is undefined.  
- **VeryGood**: Strong positive chemistry.  
- **Good**: Positive and friendly relationship.  
- **Normal**: Neutral or average compatibility.  
- **Bad**: Tension or conflict present.  
- **VeryBad**: Extremely poor compatibility or animosity.
