## 🎰 Enum: EB1SlotType

Specifies the type of slot used in various systems such as interactions, objects, or visual/audio effects.

- **None** (`0`): No specific slot type assigned.
- **Interaction**: Slot used for interaction-related elements.
- **Object**: Slot used for placing objects.
- **Fx**: Slot reserved for visual effects (FX).
- **ExtraRequireSpace**: Slot that requires additional physical or logical space.
- **Audio**: Slot designated for sound or audio sources.
