## 🧬 Enum: EB1BodyAgeType

Defines the age stage of a character's body used in customization or animation selection.

- **Invalid**: Represents an undefined or invalid age type.  
- **Default**: Uses the default age configuration.  
- **Adult**: A fully grown character body.  
- **Youth**: A teenage or adolescent body type.  
- **Baby**: An infant or toddler body type.
