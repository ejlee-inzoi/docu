## 🎨 Enum: EB1AppearanceParts

Defines individual parts of a character's appearance used for clothing, hair, and body configuration.

- **Body**: The base mesh of the character body.  
- **Head**: The head part, including face mesh.  
- **Top**: Outer top clothing, such as shirts or blouses.  
- **Bottom**: Bottom clothing like pants or skirts.  
- **Shoes**: Footwear such as shoes or boots.  
- **Jacket**: Outerwear such as jackets or coats.  
- **Hat**: Headwear including caps or helmets.  
- **Socks**: Socks or similar accessories for the feet.  
- **InnerTop**: Inner top clothing worn under outer tops.  
- **InnerUnder**: Underwear bottoms worn beneath outerwear.
- **Hair**: Main hairstyle mesh.  
- **Garment**: Additional garments like cloaks or dresses.  
- **FaceHair**: Facial hair such as beards or mustaches.
