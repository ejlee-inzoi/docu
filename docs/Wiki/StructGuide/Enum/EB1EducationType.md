## 🎓 Enum: EB1EducationType

Represents the level of formal education attained by a character.

- **Invalid**: Undefined or not applicable.  
- **Elementary**: Primary school level education.  
- **Middle**: Middle school level.  
- **High**: High school graduate or equivalent.  
- **College**: College or university education.  
- **Max**: Upper bound marker (not typically used in logic).
