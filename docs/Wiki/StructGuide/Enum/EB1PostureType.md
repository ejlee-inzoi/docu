## 🎛️ Enum: EB1PostureType

Represents different physical postures for characters.

- **Stand**: Standing posture.  
- **Sit**: Sitting posture.  
- **Sleep**: Sleeping or lying in bed.  
- **Vehicle**: Seated in a vehicle.  
- **Lay**: To place a baby in a lying position, often referring to gently laying the baby down on a surface.  
- **Carried**: Being held by another character, typically used when a baby is picked up and held in their arms.
