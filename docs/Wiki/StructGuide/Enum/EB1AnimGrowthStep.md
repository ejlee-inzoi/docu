## 🧒 Enum: EB1AnimGrowthStep

Represents the growth stage of a character for animation purposes.  
Used to determine which animations apply to a character based on their life stage.

- **Default**: Uses the default growth stage.
- **Adult**: Grown character capable of performing all standard animations.
- **Youth**: A teenage or adolescent character with limited adult behaviors.
- **Baby**: Infant stage; typically involves crawling or idle animations.
- **Elderly**: A senior character with slower or unique animations.
- **Infant**: A newborn stage, distinct from baby, possibly used for carried or crib-bound behavior.
