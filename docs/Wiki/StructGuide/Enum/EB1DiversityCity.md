## 🏙️ Enum: EB1DiversityCity

Represents culturally distinct fictional cities used for diversity simulation.

- **Invalid**: Represents an undefined or unassigned city.  
- **Dowon**: A fictional city representing Korean culture.  
- **Blissbay**: A fictional city inspired by North American culture.  
- **Cahaya**: A fictional city representing Southeast Asian or tropical culture.  
- **Max**: Internal marker, typically unused in logic.
