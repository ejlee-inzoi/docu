## 🎛️ Enum: EB1OutfitCategory

Defines outfit categories used for wardrobe management.

- **Everyday**: Daily wear.  
- **Formal**: Formal or dressy wear.  
- **School**: School uniform.  
- **Sleep**: Sleepwear.  
- **Party**: Party or celebration outfit.  
- **Sports**: Sportswear or activewear.  
- **Swimwear**: Swimwear.  
- **Summer**: Summer clothing.  
- **Winter**: Winter clothing.
