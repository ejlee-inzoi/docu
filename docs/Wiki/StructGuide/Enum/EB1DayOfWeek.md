## 📅 Enum: EB1DayOfWeek

Represents days of the week, used for scheduling, routines, or time-based conditions.

- **Monday**: Monday.  
- **Tuesday**: Tuesday.  
- **Wednesday**: Wednesday.  
- **Thursday**: Thursday.  
- **Friday**: Friday.  
- **Saturday**: Saturday.  
- **Sunday**: Sunday.  
- **Invalid**: Represents an undefined or invalid day.
