## 🎛️ Enum: EB1SiteActionReason

Represents categorized options used throughout simulation and logic systems.

- **Invalid**: Indicates an invalid or uninitialized value.  
- **Tax**: The action is triggered due to unpaid taxes.  
- **Fire**: The action is triggered because of a fire emergency.  
- **Repair**: The action is triggered to initiate repair procedures.  
- **SiteActionTrigger**: Triggered by a specific site action condition.  
- **Death**: The action was triggered by a character's death.
