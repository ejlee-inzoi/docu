## ΏθπΣέΑΞΏθνζέΑΞΏθπΣ Enum: EB1SiteEventTargetRoleOption

Specifies role selection preferences when assigning a target in a site event.

- **None** (`0`): No role preference.
- **Any**: No gender or role preference; any participant can be selected.
- **PreferMale**: Prefer selecting a male participant when possible.
- **PreferFemale**: Prefer selecting a female participant when possible.
