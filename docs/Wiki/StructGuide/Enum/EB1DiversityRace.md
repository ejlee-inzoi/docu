## 🧬 Enum: EB1DiversityRace

Represents racial classifications used in diversity simulations and character generation.

- **Invalid**: Undefined or unspecified race.  
- **Caucasian**: European or Western racial appearance.  
- **AfricanAmerican**: African or African-American heritage.  
- **LatinAmerican**: Latin American background.  
- **EastAsian**: East Asian racial appearance (e.g., Korean, Japanese, Chinese).
