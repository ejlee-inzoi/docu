## 🎛️ Enum: EB1ServiceVisitType

Represents categorized options used throughout simulation and logic systems.

- **None**: Indicates that no specific value is selected.  
- **Personal**: Specifies that the service visit is tied to a character. The destination site is the character's current location..  
- **Site**: The service is tied to a specific site. The destination is also the character's current site.  
- **SiteEvent**: The visit is triggered by a site event. The destination is determined by the character's current scheduled site event.
