## 🪄 Struct: FB1Filter_AreaCylinder

Defines a cylindrical area filter, typically used for 3D spatial checks, with optional inner radius and defined height.

### Properties

- **Radius** (`float`): Outer radius of the cylindrical area.
- **InnerRadius** (`float`): Optional inner radius that defines an excluded core.
- **Height** (`float`): Vertical height of the cylindrical volume.
