## 🧩 Struct: FB1RelationshipFxSet

Defines visual FX used when a relationship event begins and ends.

### Properties

- **StartFxAsset** (`TSoftObjectPtr<UNiagaraSystem>`): Niagara FX played at the start of the relationship event.  
- **EndFxAsset** (`TSoftObjectPtr<UNiagaraSystem>`): Niagara FX played at the end of the relationship event.
