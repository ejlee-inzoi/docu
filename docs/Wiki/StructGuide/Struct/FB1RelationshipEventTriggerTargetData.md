## 🧩 Struct: FB1RelationshipEventTriggerTargetData

Defines conditions for either the self or target in a relationship event trigger.

### Properties

- **Type** (`EB1FamilyRelationshipType`): Type of family relationship to evaluate.  
- **Gender** (`EB1GenderType`): Gender requirement for this participant.  
- **Probability** (`float`): The likelihood that this participant will be selected as the initiator of the event.
