# 📘 LevelQuality Data Guide

## 🧩 Struct: FB1LevelQualityTableRow

Defines quality weighting across different levels, often used in crafting.

### Properties

- **Id** (`FName`): Unique identifier for this level-quality entry.  
- **QualityWeights** (`TArray<FB1QualityWeight>`): Maps level differences to the percentage distribution of quality outcomes..
